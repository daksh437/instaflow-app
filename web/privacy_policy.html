1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. This information is necessary for account creation, authentication, and account management.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information such as your display name and profile photo, which is stored in your account profile.

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • App Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features you use, content you generate, and actions you take within the App. This helps us improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Crash Reports and Logs: We automatically collect crash reports and error logs to help us identify and fix technical issues, improve app stability, and enhance performance. This includes information about app crashes, errors, and performance metrics.
  • Analytics Data: We collect analytics data to understand how users interact with the App, including session duration, feature usage, and user engagement metrics.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication: When you sign in using email and password, authentication is handled by Google Firebase, which may collect authentication-related data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services: If you make in-app purchases, Google Play processes your payment information. We do not collect or store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create, maintain, and manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide you with access to the App's features.
  • App Functionality: To provide, operate, and maintain the App's core features, including AI-powered content generation (captions, reel scripts, hashtags, content ideas), and to personalize your experience.
  • Analytics and Performance Monitoring: To analyze app usage patterns, monitor performance, identify and fix bugs, improve app stability, and enhance user experience.
  • Advertising: To display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob, which helps us provide the App as a free service.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide technical support, and address any issues you may encounter.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, security threats, or other malicious activities.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App, including Google Firebase (for authentication and analytics), Google AdMob (for advertising), and Google Play (for in-app purchases). These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

Important: We do not share your personal data (such as email address) directly with advertisers. Advertising is handled through Google AdMob, which uses anonymized data for ad targeting as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

InstaFlow uses the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

4.1 Google Firebase

  • Firebase Authentication: Handles user authentication and account management. Data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and error logs to help us improve app stability. This service is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics: Provides analytics data about app usage and performance.

4.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use data for ad personalization and measurement. You can learn more about how Google uses data for advertising in Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Google Play Services

For in-app purchases and subscription management, we use Google Play Billing. Payment processing is handled by Google, and we do not collect or store your payment information.

Note: These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

5. Advertising

InstaFlow displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID and other anonymized data to show you relevant ads. AdMob's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Please note that opting out of personalized advertising does not eliminate ads from the App; you will still see advertisements, but they may be less relevant to your interests.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • Secure Authentication: User authentication is handled through Google Firebase, which uses industry-standard security practices.
  • Access Controls: We limit access to your personal information to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job functions.
  • Regular Security Updates: We regularly update our security practices and infrastructure to address emerging threats.

Important: While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with the App's services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7.1 Account Data

We retain your account information (email address, profile data) for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 7 business days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

7.2 Analytics and Crash Data

Analytics data and crash reports are retained for up to 90 days to help us improve the App. After this period, the data is automatically deleted or anonymized.

7.3 Requesting Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by sending an email to instaflow38@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My InstaFlow Account" and your registered email address. We will process your request and delete your data within 7 business days.

Important: Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. We do not offer partial data deletion without deleting your entire account.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access and review your account information through the App's profile settings.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your account information at any time through the App's settings.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at instaflow38@gmail.com.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's advertising settings as described in Section 5.
  • Uninstall: You can uninstall the App at any time from your device. Uninstalling the App does not automatically delete your account data; you must request account deletion separately.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at instaflow38@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
