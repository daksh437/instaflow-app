InstaFlow – Account & Data Deletion

Introduction

At InstaFlow, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your account and data. You can request deletion of your InstaFlow account and all associated data at any time.

Steps to Delete Account

To delete your InstaFlow account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at instaflow38@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete My InstaFlow Account"
  3. In the email body, please mention your registered email ID that you used to create your InstaFlow account
  4. Our support team will process your request and confirm the deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User account information (email address and profile details)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Generated content history (captions, hashtags, bio, etc.)
  • All stored preferences and settings
  • Subscription and payment information

Data Retention Policy

Important: Once you request account deletion, all your personal data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days of receiving your request. No personal data is retained after the deletion process is complete.

Please note that some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes as required by law, but this will not include any personally identifiable information.

Contact Information

For account deletion requests or any questions regarding your data, please contact us:

Support Email:
instaflow38@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all deletion requests within 48 hours and complete the deletion process within 7 business days.
